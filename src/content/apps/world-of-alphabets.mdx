**World of Alphabets** is a free learning app for curious minds who want to understand how humans write. It’s part atlas, part flashcard trainer, and part rabbit hole, built for quick sessions that add up fast.

## Features

### Vast choice of scripts

Explore **267 writing systems** and **2400+** characters across them. You’ll find ancient scripts, modern alphabets, and even constructed writing systems from _The Lord of the Rings_, _Star Wars_, and more.

### Learn the story behind the alphabets

Get the context that makes a script memorable: origins, regions, key traits, and the languages that use it.

### Alphabet overview

See each character at a glance: the sign, its name, transliteration, and typical pronunciation.

### Flashcards

Learn at your own pace with flashcards, plus stroke order hints and common pronunciations.

### Script Map

Navigate a world map of scripts. The historical birthplaces of all **267** entries are pinned for effortless exploration.

### Languages

Explore **629 languages**, real and constructed, and the writing systems they use.

### Dark mode

Read comfortably day or night with a polished dark theme designed for long sessions.

### Tailored quiz

Turn curiosity into progress with multiple-choice quizzes. You can also build custom quizzes around the scripts you’re learning.
