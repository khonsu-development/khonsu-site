Discover **World of Alphabets**: a free mobile app designed to teach writing systems from all around the world.

## Features

### Vast choice of scripts

Learn about **267+ writing systems**, and the signs for **54** of them. Discover constructed scripts and fictitious alphabets from _The Lord of the Rings_, _Star Wars_, and more.

### Learn the story behind the alphabets

Discover each alphabet's history, characteristics and related languages. Each script is a fascinating world!

### Alphabet overview

Get an instant overview of each alphabet: the letter, its name, its transliteration and its sound in a glance.

### Flashcards

Use flashcards to learn each character at your own pace, including how to write it by hand, and how it's most often pronounced.

### Script Map

Navigate a world of scripts. The birthplaces of all **267+ scripts** have been meticulously added to this map.

### Languages

Learn about **628+ languages**, real and constructed, and which writing systems they use.

### Dark mode

Experience sleek elegance and eye comfort with the dark theme. Reduce eye strain, save battery, and enjoy a modern interface tailored for night-time or all-day use.

### Tailored quiz

Challenge yourself to a multiple-choice quiz to learn each sign. You can even design your own quizzes.
