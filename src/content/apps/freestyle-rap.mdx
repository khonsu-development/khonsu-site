Up your freestyle rapping game — a simple app to learn how to rap freestyle, with inspiration.

“There’s no reason for somebody who’s good at writing rap to be good at freestyling. They’re different parts of your brain.” — Daveed Diggs

Freestyle Rap is powered by [Double Rhyme](https://double-rhyme.com/), the rapper’s reference for complex rhyme generation.

## How it works

### 1. Choose your favorite beat

Browse and purchase beats from a large library.

### 2. Get ready

Pick a language, beat, and tempo.

### 3. Rap your freestyle

Rhymes and pictures appear on tempo to guide your flow.

### 4. Review your recording

Review and share performances — or keep them private.
