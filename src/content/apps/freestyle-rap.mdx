**Freestyle Rap** is a practice studio in your pocket. Pick a beat, set the tempo, and get timed prompts that help you keep moving, so you can build flow, comfort, and consistency.

“There’s no reason for somebody who’s good at writing rap to be good at freestyling. They’re different parts of your brain.” - Daveed Diggs

Freestyle Rap is powered by [Double Rhyme](https://double-rhyme.com/), the rapper’s reference for complex rhyme generation.
