World of Communism offers an end-to-end learning experience on Communism, Socialism, and Anarchism — from ideas and personalities to movements and writings.

“The first duty of a man is to think for himself” — Jose Marti

## Features

### Treasure trove of information

Discover **140+ key** Communist and Anarchist personalities, plus dozens of movements and writings — neatly organised.

### Meet the vanguard

Explore philosophers, artists, and politicians — from Marx to Goldman — with context on ideas, struggles, and impact.

### Interactive map

Learn how different countries engaged with communist and anarchist histories.

### Music for a revolution

Discover left-wing hymns and songs from around the world — with lyrics.

### Category quizzes

Quiz yourself on movements, influential figures, songs, and books.

### Tailored quizzes

Design quizzes that match what you’re studying — great for beginners and enthusiasts.

### Navigate movements

Understand distinctions between movements (from Ethical Socialism to Green Anarchism).

### Dark theme

Comfortable reading with a sleek dark UI.
