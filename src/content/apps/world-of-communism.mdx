**World of Communism** is a structured learning app for modern political history and theory. It’s built to help you understand the ideas, the vocabulary, and the real-world movements, without getting lost.

“The first duty of a man is to think for himself” - Jose Marti

Discover **140+ key** communist and anarchist personalities, plus dozens of movements and writings, neatly organized for fast browsing and deeper study.
