Welcome to **World of Dinosaurs** — a tribute to all the animals that roamed our planet before us.

Introducing World of Dinosaurs: an Android application packed with information, fun quizzes, and features to help you explore dinosaurs and prehistoric animals — from famous species to lesser-known giants.

## Features

### Broad list of animals

Discover **1900+ prehistoric animals**: dinosaurs, pterosaurs, aquatic titans, plus extinct animals of the Cenozoic.

### Extensive information

Detailed descriptions, lifelike images, size comparisons, habitat info, and trivia.

### Go back in time

Explore billions of years of Earth history with an interactive Geological Time Scale.

### Articles

Stories spanning prehistoric life, extinction, fossils, and new research.

### Interactive quizzes

Quizzes designed for beginners and experts alike — challenge your memory and deepen your understanding.

### Tailored quiz

Customize quizzes: match names, identify images, or focus on fascinating facts.

### Learn about your favourite animals

Different quizzes for different categories of prehistoric animals.

### Discovery sites

Explore fossil digging sites around the world — including animals found near you.

### Dark mode

A polished dark theme for comfortable reading day or night.
